Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can listen to Glow FM through our website by clicking the "Listen Live" button, downloading our mobile app (available on iOS and Android), or using your favorite radio streaming app by searching for "Glow FM 99.1".

You can request songs through our website contact form, by calling our request line at +234 703 022 3281 or by sending us a message on our social media channels. Make sure to include the song title and artist name!
