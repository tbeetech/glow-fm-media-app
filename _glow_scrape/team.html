Mojisola Oladele (arpa)

Marketing

Management

Mojisola Oladele (arpa) is a seasoned broadcaster with over 28 years of professional experience in the media and communications industry. She built a distinguished career at the Ondo State Radiovision Corporation (OSRC), where she served diligently from 1997 to 2018, contributing significantly to broadcasting excellence before her voluntary retirement. Her academic background reflects a strong blend of creativity, communication, and strategic thinking. She holds a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Graphics Design from The Polytechnic, Ibadan, Oyo State, as well as a Master’s degree in Communication and Language Arts from the University of Ibadan. She is also an Associate Member of the Advertising Regulatory Council of Nigeria (ARCON), underscoring her professional standing in advertising and media regulation. Currently, Mojisola Oladele serves as the Senior Marketing Manager at Glow 99.1 FM, located in Ijapo Estate, Akure, where she brings her vast experience in broadcasting, branding, and strategic communication to drive the station’s marketing initiatives and audience growth.
